Here are some of my poems:

O Heirs of those that were burnt

Keep your lineage carefully hidden

Bide your time, keep allies close

Hide your strength for they still hunt.


Yet seeds of disarray are already sown

Their Waterloo heretofore written

The time of retaliation draws close

Save friend from foe before they drown.

-Divyamaan Bhati

Oh sweet Eleftheria

Whence shall thee gaze upon me

In midnight ravings and daylight flailings

My heart only yearns for thee.


Oh boundless Eleftheria

Where shall I espy thou

On treacherous paths or smooth roads

My eyes forage for a glance at thou


Oh bashful Eleftheria

What must thy touch feel like

Shall thee guide me or shall thee consume me

Oh Eleftheria! Oh Eleftheria!

-Divyamaan Bhati
